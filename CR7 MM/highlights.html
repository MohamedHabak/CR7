Cristiano Ronaldo is a professional football player from Portugal. He was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Ronaldo started playing football at a young age and joined Andorinha when he was just eight years old. He then moved on to Nacional before joining Sporting CP's youth academy when he was just 12 years old.
